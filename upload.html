Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does rendering take?

Standard delivery is 5-7 business days. Express delivery (2-3 days) and priority (24 hours) options are available for urgent projects.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept SketchUp (.skp), Revit (.rvt), 3ds Max (.max), FBX, OBJ, DWG, reference images, and ZIP archives.

Can I request revisions?

Yes! Every project includes 2 rounds of revisions. Unlimited revisions are available as an add-on option.

How do I track my project status?

Once submitted, you can track your project progress in the User Dashboard, receive notifications, and provide feedback on drafts.

Need Help with Your Upload?

Our team is ready to assist you with any questions about file formats or project requirements.
